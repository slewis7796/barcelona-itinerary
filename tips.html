🚍 Transport

Ticket: T-usual card (Zone 1) – Unlimited metro, bus, tram & RENFE within city

🎫 Buy from airport or metro vending machines

Nearest Metro: Collblanc (L5), Torrassa (L1/L9S)

📱 Download the TMB app for maps & schedules

🚇 Airport Metro (L9S) connects directly to the city

🍴 Food & Dining

🕑 Lunch: 2:30pm is typical, ask for menu del día

🕖 Dinner: 7:30pm is early — most restaurants fill up around 8–9pm

🍽️ Popular tapas street: Carrer Blai (Poble Sec)

📱 Book ahead for weekend evenings!

🏖️ Beaches & Leisure

Best beaches for families: Nova Icària, Bogatell

🚿 Bring towel, sunscreen, water bottle

📵 Don’t leave valuables unattended — pickpocketing can happen

📶 Connectivity & Safety

📡 Public WiFi: Look for "Barcelona WiFi" in metro stations & plazas

📱 eSIM or roaming data recommended if moving around a lot

🚨 Stay alert on La Rambla and in metro stations
